How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to apply for admission to Bright Horizon Academy:

Step 1: Application Form

Fill out the online application form with accurate details about the student and parent/guardian.

Step 2: Document Submission

Submit required documents, including birth certificate, previous school records, and proof of address.

Step 3: Interview & Assessment

Attend the scheduled interview and assessment to help us understand your child better.

Step 4: Fee Payment

Pay the admission fee to secure your child's spot at Bright Horizon Academy.

Admissions Criteria

We evaluate each application based on the following criteria:

Academic Performance

We review the student's academic performance in previous school years.

Entrance Assessment

Performance in our entrance assessment is an important consideration.

Seat Availability

Admissions depend on the availability of seats in the requested grade.
